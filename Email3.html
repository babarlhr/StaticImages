The Pakistan Software Export Board (PSEB) aims to establish 250 e-Rozgaar Centers (Co-working Space) across Pakistan under the PSDP project titled “Prime Minister's Initiatives - Support for IT Startups, Specialized IT Trainings, and Venture Capital,” This initiative is designed to empower freelancers, boost productivity, and position Pakistan as a leading global digital hub.

In order to have a better understanding about co-working space preferences we are conducting a survey. This insight will help to enhance our understanding about needs of Freelancer Community and to streamline our efforts in that direction.

 

Basic Facilities offered in our e-Rozgaar center are:


Skill Development Training (Supported by PSEB when required)

Dedicated Co-working Space
CCTV Monitoring
High Speed Internet

Meeting Rooms

Private Booth

Business Class Printers

Access to Public Transportation

24/7 Building Access Dedicated Line Number

Mail & Package Handling

Complimentary Coffee & Tea

Professional & Social Events

Front Desk Service

Mailing Address

Document Services

Administrative Support Services

Power Back up
